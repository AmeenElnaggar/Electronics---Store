RESULTS

The Dive In Serum is a hydrating and soothing serum with a light, watery texture that leaves a fresh, non-greasy finish. Formulated without fragrance, its scent is naturally fresh.

Formulated with a complex of 5 types of hyaluronic acid, it boosts skin elasticity and reduces dryness, while preserving moisture and protecting against dehydration. Enriched with ceramides, it helps to prevent dehydration and strengthen the skin barrier while soothing. Finally, madecassoside soothes and repairs the skin.

The skin is deeply and evenly moisturised. It is plumped, soft and supple. Soothed and calmed, irritation and redness are reduced, the skin barrier is strengthened, and the complexion is brighter and more radiant.
